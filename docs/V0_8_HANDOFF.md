## First Scenario

**Priority Part Request — Readiness at Risk**

A unit submits a priority requirement for a part. The dashboard says supply is available, but the evidence is incomplete. The player must determine whether the requirement is valid, whether the inventory record is trustworthy, whether distribution can meet the timeline, and whether a workaround creates audit or readiness risk.
